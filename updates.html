@studybliss.official on September 12, 2024 · Updates Horizon for studybliss



Need volunteer experiences? Well, you're just in luck! The studybliss team is recruiting Academic Patrons for CAIE IGCSE and IBDP!

An Academic Patron is required to provide various resources on the subjects they've been assigned to, create social media content scripts and always do tasks punctually.

Resources must be legal (no plagiarism and copyright violations), complete, beneficial and following the current syllabus. They can provide digital notes, handwritten notes, images, videos, audios, websites, examples, worksheets and flashcards. They can also create content scripts for how to study and ace a subject, a soft skill (for Soft Skills Camps), explanations, personal experiences and advice and other topics. They are also allowed to design their own content's posts!

Fill in this form to apply! (Due on 16 September 2024, 23.59, UTC +07.00)
